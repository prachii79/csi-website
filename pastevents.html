Past Events

Magic of Mathematics

To get fun with mathematical magic, CSI-TCET organized an interaction session with one of the mathematical genius, Ms. Akshita Shah.She represented India in Mathematics and has added several trophies and medals to her name.

Smart India Hackathon 2020

CSI-TCET organized an interaction session with the winners of Smart India Hackathon 2020. The aim of the session was to make the students aware of the culture of hackathon and vital winning tips. The team also shared their challenges and experiences

Machine Learning Workshop

The Machine Learning two days workshop was conducted online through the Zoom application by the speaker Mr. Nikhil John. The emphasis was laid on the fundamental topics of Machine Learning such as Linear regression, Logistics regression, etc

Project Exhibition

CSI-TCET organized a project exhibition where students of our computer department got a perfect stage to present their project based on intriguing ideas in their respective field The session benefited the students about the confidence to present their ideas

Workshop on Kotlin

CSI - TCET organized a Webinar on Android App Development using Kotlin to provide an overview of Application development. The speaker Mr. Vishal Jha from Edudictive - one of India’s pioneering institutions gave a good hands-on session on Kotlin.

Link-in with LinkedIn

The speaker explained how to set up the LinkedIn profile and what details should be added to it.She also focused on little things like Open to Work or Recruitment, Sending a note while connecting, Linkedin algorithm of 1st, 2nd and 3rd circle, etc.

IMS: Understanding The GRE

CSI - TCET organized a webinar with IMS Learning, to guide, counsel and prepare the students for various entrance tests. The speaker Mr. Shashank Prabhu from IMS - one of India’s pioneering institutions in education, enlightened the students of the various opportunities available for higher studies.

Yocket:Global Admit Application Stategies for College Aspirants

CSI - TCET in collaboration with well-known Overseas Education Consultant, Yocket conducted a webinar on "Cost of Studying abroad". The speaker was Mrs. Biruda Virkud, an education expert with more than 20 years of experience.

Spin The wheel

This individual gaming activity where each player virtually spinned a wheel consisted of a range of activities and rewards. The participants either performed a task and so were rewarded. This event turned out to be amusing and rewarding for the participants
